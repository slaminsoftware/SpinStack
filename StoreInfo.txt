Full Description:

SpinStack — Twist · Drop · Match
Think you're good at color puzzles? SpinStack has a third dimension that says otherwise.
Each row of neon bricks has four hidden sides — and every side could be the key to your next clear. Tap bricks to shift their colors, swipe to spin the stack, and match all six bricks in a row to make it vanish. Sounds simple. It isn't.
Every few taps, a new row drops. The stack keeps growing. The beat picks up. And suddenly you're spinning the cube frantically trying to find that one matching side before it all falls apart.
Power-ups keep things spicy:
💣 Bomb — blow up an entire row instantly
⚡ Lightning — zap the row below yours
🌈 Rainbow — paint a whole row your dominant color
⭐ Star — pocket a quick 500-point bonus
❄️ Freeze — buy yourself a few extra taps of breathing room
Clear a board? Prove it wasn't luck.
Between waves, a bonus challenge drops — and it tests a completely different skill. Drop pieces onto hidden targets from memory. Match incoming colors to the right column before the timer kills your streak. Tap a sequence of columns in the exact order you memorized. Nail it and carry bonus points into the next wave. Fail and carry the shame.
Features:
• 5 difficulty levels — from chill to flat-out Insane
• Endless wave mode — each cleared board gets one row harder
• 3 rotating inter-wave bonus challenges — Blind Drop, Color Gauntlet, Sequence Drop
• Efficiency bonuses for clearing rows in 3 taps or fewer
• 6 achievements to unlock
• Colorblind-friendly mode
• Procedurally synthesized music that gets tenser as the danger rises
• No internet required
One more wave. You've got this.

Subtitle (30 chars):
Neon cube color puzzle
Description:

The stack is rising. The music is thumping. Can you spin fast enough?
SpinStack is a neon color-matching puzzle with a twist — literally. Every row of bricks wraps around a cube with four sides, and only one side is ever visible at a time. Swipe to rotate, tap to recolor, and match all six bricks in a row to clear it before the stack hits the top.
It starts easy. It does not stay easy.
What makes SpinStack different:
Every row is a 3D object. The colors you need might be hiding on the back. Spin to find them, match to clear them, and race the ever-dropping rows to stay alive.
Power-up bricks spawn mid-game and change everything:
— 💣 Bomb: instant row destruction
— ⚡ Lightning: obliterates the row below
— 🌈 Rainbow: floods a row with its dominant color
— ⭐ Star: 500-point windfall
— ❄️ Freeze: delays the next incoming row
Between every wave, a bonus challenge.
Clearing a board earns you a challenge — three of them, rotating in sequence, each one testing something the main game doesn't. Blind Drop hides the targets after a brief reveal window and makes you drop from memory. Color Gauntlet fires colored pieces at you one by one and punishes hesitation. Sequence Drop shows you a column order for a few seconds, then goes dark — get it right and bank the bonus. Get it wrong and watch the points evaporate.
Go deeper:
Five difficulty levels from Easy to Insane. Each cleared board feeds straight into the next wave — same level, one more starting row. The further you go, the less time you have. The music knows.
Colorblind mode included. No account needed. No nonsense.
