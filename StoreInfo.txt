SpinStack — Twist · Drop · Match
Think you're good at color puzzles? SpinStack has a third dimension that says otherwise.

Neon cube color puzzle
Description:

The stack is rising. The music is thumping. Can you spin fast enough?
SpinStack is a neon color-matching puzzle with a twist — literally. Every row of bricks wraps around a cube with four sides, and only one side is ever visible at a time. Swipe to rotate, tap to recolor, and match all six bricks in a row to clear it before the stack hits the top.
It starts easy. It does not stay easy.
What makes SpinStack different:
Every row is a 3D object. The colors you need might be hiding on the back. Spin to find them, match to clear them, and race the ever-dropping rows to stay alive.
Power-up bricks spawn mid-game and change everything:
— 💣 Bomb: instant row destruction
— ⚡ Lightning: obliterates the row below
— 🌈 Rainbow: floods a row with its dominant color
— ⭐ Star: 500-point windfall
— ❄️ Freeze: delays the next incoming row
Go deeper:
Five difficulty levels from Easy to Insane. Each cleared board feeds straight into the next wave — same level, one more starting row. The further you go, the less time you have. The music knows.
Colorblind mode included. No account needed. No nonsense.
